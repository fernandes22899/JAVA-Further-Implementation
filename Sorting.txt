Bubble Sort-Swaps elements to put in order. Large values towards top and low values towards bottom. O(n^2)
Selection Sort- select smallest and move it to the front. O(n^2)
Insertion Sort- Goes through each value in input and sorts it in the end. O(n^2)
Merge Sort- Breaks down to groups and sorts those groups. O(n log n)
Quicksort/Split Sort- Chooses values to pivot around until sorted.O(n log n)
